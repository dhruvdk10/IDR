About The Institute

The Institute of Digital Risk (IDR) is an industry-led institute focused on education, research and professional development in digital risk, cybersecurity and AI governance. IDR works with academic partners and industry experts to develop practical skills required to manage technology risks in modern organisations and critical sectors.
